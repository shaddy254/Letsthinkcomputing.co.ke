INTRODUCTION TO COMPUTERS

A computer is an electronic device that accepts data as input,process it to give the required output as information under the influence of commands and instructions. A computer is generally refered to as an electronic device since it utilizes electronic signals throughout its operations.
The first computer was developed by charles Babbage in 1837 which used punch cards as its Read only Memory, since then many other people and companies have stood in the development of more superior and digital computers used today to perform various multiple tasks.

Qualities of a computer

  • It is automatic- a computer can work with minimum human intervention provided that it is supplied with instructions and data.
  • It is a data Processor - a computer is a data processor since it processes raw facts and figures to produce information as output.
  • It is a storage device - a computer is a storage device since it stores information on the permanet storage memories for future reference.
  • It is electrical- a computer is electrical since it utilizes electrical signals throughout its operations.

Avantages of computers

  • Speed-computers can perform/work at a very high speed performing complex jobs as compared to human beings.
  • Volume -Computers can perform huge amount of work at the same time
  • Accuracy - computer work is more accurate as compared to work done by human labour.
  • Versatility- Computers can work for a long period of time without being tired or worn out.
  • Secrecy-Data stored in a computer is private to the user as it is secured through the use of passwords.
  • Efficient - computers are efficient since they save on time and space as compared to human beings.
  • Communication - computers provide communication over the internet through websites and softwares installed in it like facebook,gmail,twitter,whatsapp
  • Multi-tasking - computers can perform multiple processes at the same time using the same micro-processor.

Disadvantages of computers

  • Cost- computers are very expensive to purchase and also the overall maintenance time of the computer is very high.
  • Computer breakdown - computer breakdown may lead to the breakdown of the entire work progress in a company and hence wasting time while waiting for them to be repaired.
  • Health Hazard- prolonged use of computers without following the set procedures may lead to some health related problems like eyesight problems and back aches.
  • Reduction of labour force - Introduction of computers into a organization may lead to unemployment since computers replace human labour provided by people.
  • Moral decay- Computers may lead to moral decay when used to watch ponography materials.
  • Cannot make decisions-computers cannot make decisions on their own and hence human beings are required to provide the best information ever.
  • Loss of information-computers may lead to loss of information if they are not managed well in the organization.

Components of computers

  • Hardware - these are the physical touchable components that make up a computer system.They include keyboard, system unit, monitor, printer and the mouse etc.
  • Software-these are set of instructions that make up the computer system and used to tell the computer what task to perform,computers entirely depend on these instructions and cannot perform any task without them.
  • Liveware- these are the people who work/interact with the computer,their work is to operate and respond to the computer where necessary.

Computer Experts

  • computer Operators - these are people who have done some computing software packages and know how to operate a computer but not in detail.
  • Computer Programmer - Are people who have know how on programming and can write and execute programs and aalso test them on a computer.
  • Computer Analyst/System Designer - are responsible for creating and integrating computer systems to meet the demands of an organization which may also include installation of softwares.
