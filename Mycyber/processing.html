CLASSIFICATION OF COMPUTERS

classification acccording to data proccessing

computers can be classified according to the functionality and the type of data that the computer can handle.data to be processed by a computer can either be discrete or continours in nature.In classification according to functionality we hereby get three types of computers.

  • Digital computers
  • Analogue computers
  • Hybrid computers

Digital computers

They are computers that provide data that is discrete in nature.these computers therfore perfoms calculations and operations that are reprisented in digits either 1 or 0.the Digital computers are therefore capable of performing mathematical calculations,organize and analyze data and perform othe industrial processes.A good example of these computers Digital stopwatches

Analogue computers

These are types of computers that can perfom and manipulate data that is continous in nature. These computers are said to use continous and changing aspects of pysical facts such as weather and distance. examples of computers in this classification include;

  • weight machines
  • thermometer
  • stethoscope

Hybrid computers

These are the types of computers that are said to exhibit both the analogue and the digital characteristics the computers accept data that is anaolugue in nature,convert it into digital form and then process it in a digital manner, some good examples of these computers are the computers that are found in Icu wards and also computers that are used in scientific laboratories.

classification according to purpose

computers can classified according to the purpose or ideal job they were developed to perform in this classification we get computers like the general purpose computers and special purpose computers.

special purpose

The special purpose computers are the computers that are designed to perform only one specific task. these computers cannot perform any othe task apart from that they are dedicated for.This is therefore one of the major disadvantages of these types of computers.however these computers may have the same features as the general purpose computers , there applicability to one job makes them different.special pupose computers can be used in controlling robots,traffic lights controll,satellite launch among others. due to the fact that they are dedicated to one job memory is not wasted and hence they are fast in data manipulation.

General purpose computers

These are computers that are designed to perform any task that comes on their ways.This property remains to be the main advantage of general purpose computers over the special purpose computers.The general purpose computers can perform any task provided that they are loaded with the apropriate programs,some of the tasks that can be performed by these computers include scientific calculations,inventory management,controlling security systems among others.

classification according to size

In this classification,computers are clasified according to their physical size,in this classification the computers with the largest physical size perform more complex tasks than those with small physical sizecomputers in this generation include,

  • super computers
  • main-frame computers
  • mini computers
  • micro computers
